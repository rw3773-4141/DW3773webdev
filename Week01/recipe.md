# Grilled Cheese Sandwich

## Description
A grilled cheese sandwich is made with bread and melted cheese.  
**It is quick and easy to make.**

## Servings and Time
- **Servings:** 1 sandwich
- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 5 minutes

## Ingredients
- 2 slices of bread
- 2 slices of cheese
- 1 tbsp butter

## Instructions
1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
2. Place cheese between the bread slices.
3. Cook in a pan until both sides are golden and the cheese is melted.
