A simple grilled cheese sandwich made with bread and melted cheese.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice buttered-side down on a plate.
  3. Cook in a pan until golden and melted.
