How can the same content feel completely different through design?

This project explores how layout, spacing, typography, and color can change the emotional experience of a website. The content stays similar, but the structure creates a new mood.

01

About the project

My midterm focuses on visual mood in web design. I am interested in how a website can guide emotion not only through words or images, but also through silence, emptiness, rhythm, and structure.

For this version, I am building a simple and well-designed experience with a calm and quiet feeling. The goal is to make the page feel soft, spacious, and intentional.

02

Design concept

Typography

Clean and readable type helps create clarity and emotional tone.

Spacing

Open space and careful balance help the page feel quiet and slow.

Structure

A simple layout can feel calm instead of crowded or chaotic.

03

What I am focusing on

In this version, I am paying close attention to spacing, hierarchy, and pace. I want the page to feel clear and controlled without using too many elements.

"Small design choices can quietly shape the way a page feels."
